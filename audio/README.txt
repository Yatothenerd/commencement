Put two audio files here:

  music.mp3   -> background music (loops; starts when the smoke transition begins)
  seal.mp3    -> short sound effect played the moment the wax seal is clicked

Notes
- MP3 works in all browsers. Keep music.mp3 reasonably small (a few MB).
- seal.mp3 should be short (≈0.5–1.5s): a soft thud / paper / whoosh.
- Music can only start after a click (browser autoplay rules) — it begins
  on the seal click, which is exactly when the smoke starts, so that's fine.
- The floating round button (bottom-right) pauses/resumes the music.
- If a file is missing, nothing breaks — that sound is just silent.
